    requires
        som.gap(lo, hi),
        lo.lt_spec(k),
        k.lt_spec(hi),
        som@.contains_key(*k.get()),
    ensures
        false,
{
    som.gap_means_empty(lo, hi, k);

REASON: `gap_means_empty` is external_body and derives `false` from its preconditions — if any precondition can be fabricated (e.g., via another external_body unsoundness), this becomes an unrestricted `false` oracle
