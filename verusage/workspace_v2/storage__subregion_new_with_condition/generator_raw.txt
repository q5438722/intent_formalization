```verus
    region: PersistentMemoryRegionView,
    start1: nat,
    len1: nat,

}
```
REASON: Nested subregion views should compose correctly â€” if subrange-of-subrange didn't equal a direct subrange at the composed offset, nested subregion creation would produce wrong views
