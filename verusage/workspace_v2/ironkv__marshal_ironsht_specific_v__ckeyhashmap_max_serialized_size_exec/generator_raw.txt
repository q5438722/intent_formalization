TYPE: boundary
CODE:
```verus
proof fn phi_1_max_serialized_size_fits_in_u32()
    ensures
        ckeyhashmap_max_serialized_size() <= u32::MAX as usize,
{
    reveal(ckeyhashmap_max_serialized_size);
}
```
REASON: The max serialized size (0x100000 = 1 MiB) fits in a u32, meaning the spec silently constrains hash maps to at most 1 MiB serialized — this may be too small for large hash maps and the limit is buried in an opaque constant with no documentation of why this bound was chosen
