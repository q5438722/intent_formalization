```verus
proof fn phi_1_interp_of_entry_between_external_body(d: Directory, i: nat, va: nat, pte: PTE)
    requires
        d.inv(),
        i < d.entries.len(),
        d.interp_of_entry(i).contains_pair(va, pte),
    ensures
        d.entry_base(i) <= va,
        va < d.next_entry_base(i),
        d.entry_base(i) < va + pte.frame.size,
        va + pte.frame.size <= d.next_entry_base(i),
{
    d.lemma_interp_of_entry_between(i, va, pte);
}
```
REASON: `lemma_interp_of_entry_between` is `external_body` — both VA bounds and frame size bounds within entry range are trusted without proof
