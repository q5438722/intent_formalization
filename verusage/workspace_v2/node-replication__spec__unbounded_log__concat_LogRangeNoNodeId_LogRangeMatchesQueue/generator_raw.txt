```verus
proof fn phi_5_no_node_id_ignores_extra_keys<DT: Dispatch>(

    }
}
```
REASON: Inserting a log entry at or beyond `end` doesn't affect `LogRangeNoNodeId` — tests that the predicate is insensitive to out-of-range mutations
