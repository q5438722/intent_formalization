```verus
        core != s.writes.core,
    ensures
        s.interp().pt_mem === s.pt_mem.write_seq(s.sbuf[s.writes.core]),

}
```
REASON: The rl2→rl1 refinement flattens pt_mem to the writer's view only — all non-writer cores' store buffers are invisible at rl1, meaning rl1 cannot distinguish states that differ only in non-writer sbufs
