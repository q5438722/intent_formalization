        mem1.mem[add(mem1.pml4, mul(l0_bits!(vaddr), WORD_SIZE))] == mem2.mem[add(mem2.pml4, mul(l0_bits!(vaddr), WORD_SIZE))],
    ensures

        }),

REASON: The first walk step depends only on pml4 and the L0 entry — two memories agreeing on these should produce identical first steps, testing that walk_next doesn't depend on unrelated memory
