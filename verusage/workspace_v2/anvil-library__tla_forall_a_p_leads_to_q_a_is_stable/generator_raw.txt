```verus
proof fn phi_3_always_leads_to_from_stable(p: TempPred<int>, q: TempPred<int>, ex: Execution<int>)
    requires
        valid(stable(p.leads_to(q))),
        p.leads_to(q).satisfied_by(ex),
    ensures
        always(p.leads_to(q)).satisfied_by(ex),
{
    assert(stable(p.leads_to(q)).satisfied_by(ex));
}
```
REASON: stable(X).satisfied_by(ex) should mean X(ex) ==> always(X)(ex), but extracting always from the nested closure wrapping requires SMT to resolve stable's implies/always closure chain — if this resolves incorrectly it gives always for free
