```verus
        b0.outstanding_write == Some(42u8),

        b1.outstanding_write == Some(99u8),
        b1.state_at_last_flush == 0u8,
    ensures
        ({
            let view = PersistentMemoryRegionView { state: seq![b0, b1, b1, b1, b1, b1, b1, b1, b0, b1, b1, b1, b1, b1, b1, b1] };

```
REASON: Different chunks can independently choose between flushed and committed states — one chunk crashes as committed while another crashes as flushed; if the crash model forced all-or-nothing across chunks, it would be too strong
