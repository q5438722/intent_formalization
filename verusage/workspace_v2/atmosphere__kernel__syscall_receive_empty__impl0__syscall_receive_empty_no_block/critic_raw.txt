===SUMMARY===
The single candidate is a false positive. While `is_empty` is technically vacuous when `kernel_l4_end >= 512`, this condition is unreachable: `pagetables_wf` constrains `kernel_l4_end == KERNEL_MEM_END_L4INDEX` (1) and `iommutables_wf` constrains it to 0. No well-formed `PageTable` can have `kernel_l4_end >= 512`.
===END_SUMMARY===
