proof fn phi_1_aligned_successor(a: nat, s: nat)

        aligned(a, s),

        s > 1,
    ensures
        aligned(a + b + 1, s),

}

REASON: Sum of two aligned values plus 1 should not be aligned for size > 1, since alignment is closed under addition but not under +1
