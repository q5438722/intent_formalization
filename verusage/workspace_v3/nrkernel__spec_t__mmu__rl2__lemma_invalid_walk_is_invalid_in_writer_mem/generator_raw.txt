```verus
proof fn phi_4_aligned_additive_divisors(addr: nat, s1: nat, s2: nat)
    requires
        s1 > 0,
        s2 > 0,
        aligned(addr, s1),
        aligned(addr, s2),
    ensures
        aligned(addr, s1 + s2),
{
}
```
REASON: Alignment to two sizes does not imply alignment to their sum (e.g., 12 is aligned to 4 and 6 but not to 10)
