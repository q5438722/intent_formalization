**Summary of findings:**

The primary spec gap theme is the **`next_free` allocation cursor** — it's central to the body's allocation strategy but completely absent from all specs. Gaps 1–3 and 5 all stem from this: the next-fit policy, cursor advancement on alloc, cursor lowering on clear, and cursor staleness after manual `set`. Gap 4 highlights a missing liveness guarantee in `new` (contrast with `from_raw_array`). Gaps 6–7 cover error-code granularity and a useful closed-form failure condition that the spec collapses into an existential.
